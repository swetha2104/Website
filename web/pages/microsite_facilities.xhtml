FACILITIES
Special Coaching In English
This is for students from different states who have studied in different mother tongues other than English provided sufficient number of students come forward for the same.For this purpose an experienced Guest Faculty will be engaged.The charges are to be borne by participating students.The tutors at DCE visit such schools and give spot help to students. Special coaching to underachievers, supervised study camps and night classes, special counselling for parents of underachievers are some of the features of the programme

Tutorial System

Tutorial classes will be conducted at the end of every month. The duration of the class is two hours.Appropriate questions will be framed on the portions covered till then and distributed to the students.The students and teachers of the concerned subjects will join for discussion. To explore the latent talents and to promote leadership qualities of students,the following departmental clubs function under the supervision of the departmental heads .
Hostel Facilities
Our hostel has the stylish infrastructure.This hostel is truly blessed with the best location possible.Large,well furnished and spacious rooms shared by three.pretty decent mess with pleasant ambience.24 hour security guards,Internet Connectivity..etc There are separate hostels for men and women on the campus, with all facilities for study and recreation. Besides hostel dining facility, there are food outlets and cafes too, open 12 to 15 hours a day.
Cultural Associations

Cultural programs are organized several times during the year to encourage creative pursuits and nurture talent. Students form teams and share responsibility in organizing the events. To explore the latent talents and to promote leadership qualities of students,the following departmental clubs function under the supervision of the departmental heads such as the Microzeal-(Microbiology Department) and Technokrats-(Computer Science Department), Zoorest-(Zoology Science Department) etc.
Transportation
The College offers bus service for the benefit of students coming from distant places within Bangalore-City.These buses are also used for industrial visits and short distance excursion trips.There are two vans and several cars too. Public transport buses and trains also ply to points near the campuses. The College runs a fleet of buses for transporting students to and from the city and to other areas.
